Add a dot to a note

There are two ways to add a dot to a note: Using the mouse, you can enter the Insert mode, select the dot from the variant family, and double-click on the staff where you want the dot to appear. Dots can be moved in any direction using <ctrl - up or down arrow>, <space bar> or <ctrl - space bar> . You can also add a dot by selecting the note to which it should be applied, and pressing < . > The dot will appear at the correct pitch after the note.
