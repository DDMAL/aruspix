Ligatures

There are two ligature formations available in the notes family menu. Because Aruspix functions at the graphic level, it is necessary to combine two or more separate symbols to create a ligature while you are editing a page. When the page in later exported, the separate symbols will be fused into one symbol. To create a ligature, enter the Input mode and use the mouse to select one of the two ligature symbols. Insert it onto the staff by double-clicking, and then add a breve in the same way. There will be new ligatures coming soon. . . (2 more coming)
